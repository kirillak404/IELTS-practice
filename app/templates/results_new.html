Fluency and Coherence

Feedback: You frequently used fillers and pauses that disrupt the flow of your speech. Try to think and formulate your answers more quickly.

Advice: Practice responses to typical Part 1 questions. This will help you to speak more smoothly and confidently.

Lexical Resource

Student's Speech Example: "I like my city very much. It's very nice. I like going to the park. It's very nice."

Feedback: You repetitively use the word "nice", making your speech monotonous. Try to use a more diverse vocabulary.

Advice: Try using synonyms or more complex expressions to describe your thoughts. For example, instead of "nice", you could use words like "pleasant", "relaxing", "enjoyable".

Score: 6/9

Grammatical Range and Accuracy

Student's Speech Example: "I have go to the cinema yesterday."

Feedback: There are several mistakes in using tense forms. It should be: "I went to the cinema yesterday".

Advice: Regularly practice using different grammatical structures and tense forms.

Score: 6/9

Pronunciation

Student's Speech Example: "I live in 'Mos-cow'" (pronounced as 'mos-cow' instead of 'mosco').

Feedback: There are some mistakes in pronouncing city names. In English, the city of Moscow is pronounced as 'Mosco' ('mosco').

Advice: Constant practice and listening to native speakers will help improve your pronunciation.
